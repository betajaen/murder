Bit Potion
==========

Bit Potion was a font created by Joeb Rogers licensed under the CC-BY license

It was converted by Robin Southern into the Amiga bitmap font format and is
included in this source code and releases of this game, as the the following
files:

1. BitPotion.font
2. BitPotion/9

For further information please visit:

https://joebrogers.itch.io/bitpotion
